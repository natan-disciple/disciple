We were tasked with building a women's sportswear brand from scratch that embodied elegance, aesthetics, and premium quality. We developed the brand concept, designed the clothing itself, curated the color palette, and launched a full custom online store integrated with social media.

What We Did

Naming: Developed a sleek and resonant brand name—"Nathan Anderson", alongside signature model names for each clothing line.

Branding & Socials: Established unique positioning—"Quiet Luxury Clothing." Completely designed the visual identity and VK community.

Strategy & Consulting: Structured the product matrix (from basics to premium tracksuits).

Marketing: Built an e-commerce platform with elegant UI/UX, fast-ordering, promo codes, and automated social integrations.

The Results

The brand successfully operates and ships collections nationwide. A highly recognizable trademark was established, backed by a loyal audience.

Our objective was to dismantle the stereotype of renovation being a constant stress. We packaged the studio so clients instantly recognize reliability: a place where you can confidently hand over the keys and go on vacation.

The studio is positioned as a trusted expert (150+ projects). The packaging created a waitlist of clients booked 4 to 6 weeks in advance.
